How it started...

It all began with a simple love for capturing moments—the kind that tell stories and evoke emotions. I started with just my camera and a dream, photographing sunsets, friends, and small events. Over time, my passion grew into a vision: to create a space where artistry and authenticity could come together.

As demand grew, I expanded my craft, dedicating myself to perfecting every detail and ensuring each image told a meaningful story. What started as a one-person passion project has now blossomed into a thriving boutique studio, dedicated to turning your moments into lasting memories.

WHAT WE DO?

At LensCraft Studio, we specialize in transforming life's most meaningful moments into timeless works of art. Nestled in the heart of Wellington's vibrant Cuba Street, our boutique studio is celebrated for its artistic approach and dedication to capturing the essence of every story. Whether it's the joy of a wedding day, the warmth of a family portrait, or the professionalism of a commercial shoot, we pour our creativity and passion into every frame. Our goal is to create images that not only tell your story but also evoke the emotions and memories tied to those special moments. From the soft glow of a sunset to the candid laughter of loved ones, we focus on the details that make your story unique.

We offer a range of services tailored to meet your needs, including intimate portrait sessions, wedding photography, and high-quality commercial shoots. At LensCraft, we believe in providing a seamless and personalized experience, ensuring every step—from the first consultation to the final delivery of your images—is as memorable as the moments we capture. Let us help you turn your moments into lasting memories.

Proudest Achievement: 2024 Photography Award Winner

I'm thrilled to share that LensCraft has been honored with a 2024 Photography Award! This recognition is a celebration of my dedication, creativity, and the trust my clients have placed in me over the years. It's a reminder of why I do what I do—capture the beauty of life, one moment at a time.

Every smile, every tear, and every story I've had the privilege to photograph has led me here, and I'm deeply grateful. Thank you for being part of my journey. Here's to creating more unforgettable memories together!

With love,
David
